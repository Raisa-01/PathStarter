Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a strong profile?

Make sure to complete all sections, upload a professional photo, write a compelling summary, and highlight your key skills and experiences.

Why am I not getting interview calls?

Consider updating your resume, tailoring applications to specific jobs, improving your profile visibility, and following up on applications.

How can I improve my job search results?

Use specific keywords, set up job alerts, apply early to new postings, and customize your applications for each position.

Is PathStarter free to use?

Yes, basic job searching and applying is completely free. We also offer premium features for enhanced visibility and additional tools.
